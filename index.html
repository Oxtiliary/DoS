Welcome To Your DoS & DDoS Intoduction

This is a website, put together to explain what DoS and DDoS attacks are. Hopefully this is a clear and understandable way of explaining what these types of cyber security risks are and what you can do to prevent or mitigate the effects of these cyber-attacks.

What is on this webpage

I will take you through the process of learning what DoS and DDoS attacks are page by page and how to deal with the attacks if they were to happen to you.

The Page will explain DoS and DDoS and how they are different from one another.

The

page will help you know if you are being targeted by an attacker using DoS & DDoS methods to attack your server or if your computer potentially is being used as a "zombie machine" to attack others.

The page will explain what the effects of a DoS or DDoS attack can have and the damage it causes, giving you a reason to know about them and be prepared for them.

The page will help you reduce the damage caused by a DoS and DDoS attack and even prevent it from happening to in the first place.


Conclusion

Hopefully after visiting this page, you will be more aware and have an understanding on what DoS and DDoS are. Your next step should be visiting the page to begin.
